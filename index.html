Save your progress — add this to your Home Screen

Your checked-off readings live only in this browser. If you keep using it as a regular tab instead of an installed app, your phone can quietly clear that data over time (iPhone Safari is especially aggressive about this after a week or two of inactivity) — or you could lose it simply by clearing your browsing data. That would wipe out all your progress.

Adding it to your Home Screen keeps it as its own app, so your data stays put.
